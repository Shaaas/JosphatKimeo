The Ministry

Josphat Kimeo is a Kamba gospel artist dedicated to spreading the message of Jesus Christ through traditional and contemporary worship music in Kamba, Swahili, and English.

Rooted in the spiritual heritage of Ukambani and grounded in scripture, his ministry preserves the depth of Kenyan gospel music while inviting believers everywhere into deeper faith, repentance, and devotion to God.
